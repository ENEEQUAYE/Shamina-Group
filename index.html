About Shamina Group

Shamina Group is a diverse company specializing in business consultancy, agricultural production through Shamina Farms, and hospitality services with Shamina Hotel. Established in 2015 by Muhammad Shamsu Hamidu Kabore, Shamina Group has grown to become a significant player in these sectors, leveraging its expertise and dedication to quality service. With the vision to provide exceptional consultancy services, the company expanded its operations to include Shamina Farms, focusing on sustainable agricultural practices and rental of farm machinery, and Shamina Hotel, offering top-tier hospitality services making us a trusted partner in these sectors.
